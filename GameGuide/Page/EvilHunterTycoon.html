Evil Hunter Tycoon Game Guide!

Hunter

Hunters consist of 4 classes:
Berserker, Ranger, Paladin, Sorcerer.
Each class has different skills.

Getting Hunter

You can get Hunter through Wait Slot , Hunter Invitation , Advanced Hunter Invitation , Arcane Hunter Invitation , Superior Hunter Invitation , Hero Hunter Invitation and Legendary Hunter Invitation

Wait Slot

If your Active Hunter Slot is not full, you get "One Hunter" every 10 minute. You can have 20 Active Hunters Max (At Max House Level). If the Countdown Timer reached Zero and you have Max Active Hunter (20/20 I got max house level), you have to wait 1 hour to get next hunter. And the Hunter that you got is in " Hunters Waiting. "If you have 3 Hunters in "Hunters Waiting", the countdown will disappear and you won't get hunter anymore. (The countdown continues if you banish hunter to open up slot)

Hunter Stat

HP : Decreases as hunters get attacked by monsters. Hunters can recover at the Infirmary. If their HP reaches 0, they die and must revive at the Sanctuary of Resurrection.

Satiety : Decreases every time a hunter attacks a monster. If the hunter becomes hungry, they will return to the town. They can recover their Satiety gauge at the restaurant.

Mood : Decreases every time a monster attacks a hunter. The hunter's other stats scale along with their Mood gauge. They can recover their Mood gauge at the Tavern.

Stamina : Hunter's Stamina decreases as time goes by. If the Hunter is tired they will return to the town. They can recover their Stamina at the Inn.

ATK : Affects the amount of damage the hunter inflicts on monsters. The higher the number, the more damage they can inflict.

DEF : Affects the amount of damage hunters receive from monsters. The higher the number, the lower the damage received.

CRIT : Chance to inflict 1.5x critical hit damage to monsters. (Can be increased to MAX 50% with equipment)

ATK SPD : The speed at which the hunter attacks. Lower numbers indicate faster attacks. (Can be decreased to MIN 0.25 with equipment)

Evasion : Chance to evade monster's attack. (Can be increased to MAX 40% with equipment)

Hunter Tier

Hunter tiers are divided into
Normal, Rare, Superior, Heroic, Legendary and Ultimate.
Hunter tiers are determined by their total stats.

Normal stat ( White Color ) is 0 point
High stat ( Blue Color ) is 1 point
Highest stat ( Orange Stat ) is 2 points
Ultimate stat ( Purple Stat ) is 3 points

If you calculate it and your hunter got
(21~27) point = Ultimate
(14~20) point = Legendary
(10~13) points = Heroic
(6~9) points = Superior
(2~5) points = Rare
(0~1) point = Normal
